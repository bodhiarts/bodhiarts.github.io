## 🖌️ About Me & My Art
Passionate about capturing the beauty of life through colors and shades, I am a Kerala-based painting artist working across watercolors, themes, and pencil sketches. Deeply inspired by nature, many of my works reflect the rich landscapes, heritage, and serene moods of Kerala.

Beyond my personal collections, I also create **personalized, custom artworks** for special occasions—turning your favorite memories into timeless art. 

### ✨ Core Specialties
* 💧 **Watercolors:** Vibrant landscapes, traditional Kerala architecture, and nature studies.
* ✏️ **Pencil Sketches:** Highly detailed portraits, realistic shading, and conceptual art.
* 🌅 **Thematic Paintings:** Deep, concept-driven art exploring nature and culture.
* 🎁 **Custom Commissions:** Tailor-made paintings for gifts, weddings, and anniversaries.
